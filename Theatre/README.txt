Pozorište ima jednoznacan automatski generisan celobrojan identifikator i naziv koji mogu da se dohvate. 
Može da se sastavi tekstualni opis u obliku naziv[ident]. 

Zaposleni u pozorištu je opisan imenom i pozorištem u kojem je zaposlen. 
Svi podaci mogu da se dohvate. Može da se dohvati jednoslovna oznaka vrste posla koju obavlja, 
da se sastavi tekstualni opis u obliku ime[oznaka_posla,naziv_pozorišta].

Glumac, reditelj i kostimograf su zaposleni u pozorištu. Oznaka vrste posla je G, R i K, tim redom.

Predstava je opisana nazivom, pozorištem u kojem se održava i sadrži zadat broj zaposlenih koji ucestvuju u realizaciji predstave.
Moze da se dodaju zaposleni za realizaciju predstave. 
Može da se dohvati naziv predstave, da se dohvati pozorište u kojem se održava predstava i 
da se sastavi tekstualni opis predstave navdeci naziv predstave i naziv pozorišta u kojem se predstava održava razdvojene zarezom, 
a potom zaposlene koji ucestvuju u realizaciji predstave (svakog zaposlenog u zasebnom redu).



The theater has a unique automatically generated identifier and name that can be reached
A textual description is in the form of [ident].

The employees in the theater are described by the name and the theater in which he is employed.
All data can be retrieved. A one-letter tag of the type of work it can perform can be reached
Textual description is in the form of [job_tag, name_of_the_theatre].

The actor, director and costume designer are employed in the theater. The one letter tag is A, D and C, respectively.

The performance is described by the name, the theater in which it is held and contains the given number of employees who participate
 in the realization of the performance. Employees can be added for realization of the performance.
The name of the performance can be reached, same as the theater in which the performance is held.
Textual description of the performance can be made by showing the title of the play and the name of the theater in which the performance is maintained separated by commas,
and then the employees who participate in the realization of the performance (each employee in a separate line).
